1. Data Storage Architecture

The Application does not use its own backend servers to store user-created screenshot projects or edited images.

  • Local Processing: Screenshots, selected images, edits, text overlays, crop settings, pixelation, frames, backgrounds, and exported images are processed on your device.
  • Photo Library: The Application uses Apple photo picker and photo saving APIs to let you select screenshots/images and save edited results.
  • Local Preferences: Some app settings, onboarding status, and editing preferences may be stored locally on your device.
Important: We do not have technical access to your screenshots, edited images, or exported files unless you intentionally send them to us, for example by email or support request.

2. Data We Collect and Process

2.1 User Content

User content may include:

  • Screenshots and images selected by you
  • Edited compositions
  • Text added to screenshots
  • Crop, frame, background, stitching, drawing, and pixelation edits
  • Exported images saved to your device

User content is processed locally to provide Application functionality. We do not upload your screenshots or edited images to our own servers.

2.2 Photo Library Access

The Application may request access to selected photos or permission to save edited images to your photo library.

  • The Application uses Apple’s photo picker so you can choose specific images.
  • The Application may save exported images to your photo library when you request it.
  • We do not receive or store your photo library content.

2.3 Subscription Data (RevenueCat)

Payments are processed by Apple through the App Store.

We do not access your payment card, banking, or billing details.

We use RevenueCat to manage subscription and purchase status. RevenueCat may process limited purchase-related metadata, such as:

  • Anonymous app user identifiers
  • Product identifiers
  • Entitlement status
  • Purchase, renewal, cancellation, and expiration status
  • Transaction identifiers and related subscription metadata

2.4 Wallpapers and Remote Assets

The Application may allow you to use remote wallpapers or device frame assets.

  • If you search for or load wallpapers from Unsplash, your search query and related request data may be processed by Unsplash.
  • Device frame assets or metadata may be downloaded from public asset hosting services, such as GitHub raw content.
  • These services do not receive your edited screenshots from us.

2.5 Support and Feedback

If you contact us by email or through a feedback link, we may process your email address and the content of your message in order to respond to you.
